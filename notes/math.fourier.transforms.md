$F(\omega) = \mathscr{F}\{f(t)\} = \displaystyle\int^\infty_{-\infty} f(t)e^{-j\omega t}dt$

### Inverse
$f(t) = \mathscr{F}^{-1}\{F(\omega)\} = \frac{1}{2\pi} \displaystyle\int^\infty_{-\infty} F(\omega)e^{j\omega t}dt$
