

### Euler's Formulas
$\cos x=\cfrac{e^{+jx}+e^{-jx}}{2}$   

$\sin x=\cfrac{e^{+jx}-e^{-jx}}{2 j}$

$e^{jx}=\cos x + j \sin x$  

$e^{-jx}=\cos x - j \sin x$  

$\zeta = r e^{\ j\theta}$


### Euler's Constant
For $y=e^x$, the slope of the curve at any point as well as the area from negative infinity to x equals $e^x$    
  
$\frac{d}{dx}(e^x) = e^x$  
  
$\int_{-\infty}^x e^x = e^x$
